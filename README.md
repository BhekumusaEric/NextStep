RiseHub (Working Name)

A mobile-first mentorship, opportunities, and peer-community platform built for ambitious students, graduates, interns, and early-career professionals.

Built initially with React Native.

1. The Problem

Across universities, bootcamps, internship programs, and professional communities, thousands of young people struggle with:

Not knowing where opportunities are posted
Missing application deadlines
Not having access to mentors
Feeling isolated during their growth journey
Having no trusted community to ask questions
Losing access to “plugs” (connections, referrals, resources)

Most opportunities are scattered across:

LinkedIn
WhatsApp groups
Facebook communities
Telegram channels
University mailing lists
Private networks

This creates information inequality.

People with networks win faster.

People without networks fall behind.

2. The Mission

To democratize access to:

Opportunities
Mentorship
Networks
Career knowledge
Professional communities

The goal:

Make career growth collaborative instead of isolated.
